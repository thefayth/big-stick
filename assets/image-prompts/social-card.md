# Social Card Prompt

## Filename Suggestion

`big-stick-social-card.png`

## Purpose

Open Graph / social preview image.

## Placement

Social metadata and repo preview.

## Aspect Ratio

1200x630.

## Prompt

Create an original social preview card for "Big Stick" with a protected
public/private evidence boundary theme. Use a deep ink background, warm paper
title block, graphite rails, restrained red redaction accents, and small muted
gold receipt markers. Text only: "Big Stick" and "Protected public-record and
evidence command surface." No screenshots or private data.

## Negative Prompt / Avoid

No real records, no court forms, no names, no private details, no sales CTA, no
download button, no app UI screenshot.

## Accessibility Alt Text

Big Stick social preview card with abstract redaction and receipt motifs.

## Privacy Notes

Public-safe if generated from this prompt and reviewed.
