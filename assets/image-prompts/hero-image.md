# Hero Image Prompt

## Filename Suggestion

`big-stick-hero-command-board.png`

## Purpose

Hero image for FaithCheltenham.com project page and possible public repo social
preview.

## Placement

Top of page.

## Aspect Ratio

16:9 or 3:2.

## Style Direction

Protective editorial command board; deep ink, warm paper, graphite, restrained
red, muted gold; layered abstract folders, redaction bars, timeline threads,
and soft light over an organized work surface.

## Prompt

An original abstract editorial image of a protected local command board for
public-record work and private evidence review, warm paper panels on a deep ink
desk, graphite status rails, restrained red redaction marks, muted gold
receipt tabs, archive boxes and transcript lines represented as abstract
shapes, calm serious lighting, no readable private text, no real screenshots,
high-end product story style.

## Negative Prompt / Avoid

No real documents, no names, no case numbers, no addresses, no phone numbers, no
medical records, no court forms, no police imagery, no gavel, no scales of
justice, no surveillance aesthetic, no public app interface, no celebrity or
copyrighted characters.

## Accessibility Alt Text

Abstract command-board image showing protected records, redaction marks, and
organized evidence pathways.

## Privacy Notes

Safe to generate as an original abstract visual. Requires Faith review before
publication.
