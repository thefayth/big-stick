# WordPress Section Art Prompt

## Filename Suggestion

`big-stick-section-art.png`

## Purpose

Support middle page sections without showing product screenshots.

## Placement

What this is / why it matters sections.

## Aspect Ratio

1:1 or 4:3.

## Prompt

Original abstract visual of a protected archive retrieval room connected to a
public redacted record panel. Warm paper, deep ink, graphite shelves, amber
indexing light, restrained red review marks, calm serious editorial style, no
readable private text.

## Negative Prompt / Avoid

No real documents, no private names, no court/medical forms, no faces unless
Faith separately approves portrait direction, no screenshots, no sales UI.

## Accessibility Alt Text

Abstract archive and public-record visual showing private retrieval and
reviewed public summaries.

## Privacy Notes

Public-safe prompt; final image requires review.
