# Security

Do not report sensitive issues publicly.

Do not probe, scan, scrape, attack, bypass, enumerate, or attempt to access any
private Big Stick system, worker, dashboard, storage path, repository, or
deployment target.

If you believe you found a public-surface issue, contact Faith through her
official channels with a concise, non-exploitative description. Do not include
private data, secrets, credentials, payloads, or proof-of-compromise material in
public comments or messages.

Private legal/admin/evidence workflows are not part of this public surface.
