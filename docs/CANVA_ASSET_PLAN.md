Status: READY AFTER FAITH REVIEW.

brand assets as the visual source of truth. Canva or another design tool should
polish and resize from these assets rather than replace them with generic
templates.

## Hero Image

Purpose: README hero section and FaithCheltenham.com project hero.

Dimensions: 1600 x 900.

Current asset: `assets/hero/hero-image.png`.

Style direction: tactical command board, warm paper field, dark protected panel,
red review rails, gold receipt cues.

Color direction: deep ink, warm paper, graphite, restrained red, muted gold.

Typography direction: bold geometric sans, calm spacing, no ornate legal cliche.

Canva prompt: Create a bold Big Stick hero image showing a protected
public-record command board with review, redaction, publishing, and receipt
lanes. Use deep ink, warm paper, restrained red, graphite, and muted gold.
Keep it abstract and public-safe. No real records, no faces, no court forms,
no surveillance imagery.

Alternate Canva prompt: Design a premium local-first command center hero for
Big Stick. The image should feel strategic, protective, human, and owned. Show
public/private review lanes, receipt marks, and evidence-readiness motifs
without showing private files or dashboards.

Alt text: Abstract Big Stick command-board artwork with review, redaction,
publishing, and receipt motifs.
