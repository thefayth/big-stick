# How To Engage

Big Stick is currently a protected project surface.

Appropriate public-safe engagement:

- Ask for private discussion through Faith's official channels.
- Request press or background context.
- Ask whether a future private demo, pilot, or partnership review is available.
- Respect that no product access, code access, or public workflow access is
  being offered here.

Not appropriate:

- Asking for raw records, source code, private workflows, prompts, credentials,
  legal strategy, or private screenshots.
- Treating this as a public app download.
- Scraping, mirroring, packaging, training on, or redistributing this material.
- Publishing claims about Big Stick that Faith has not approved.
