# FAQ

## Is Big Stick open source?

No. This public surface grants no license and includes no source code.

## Can I download or use Big Stick?

No public download or access path is offered here.

## Is this a legal advice tool?

No. The public description is about documentation, review, redaction, evidence
organization, transcript intake, archive retrieval, and protected workflows.

## What is Fightback?

In inspected files, Fightback is the public-record area inside Big Stick for
approved and redacted public material.

## What is Unburied?

In inspected files, Unburied is the private vault/archive area inside Big Stick.

## What is Listen Up?

In inspected files, Listen Up is the protected transcript/listening area inside
Big Stick.

## Can I contribute?

No public contribution process is open.

## How should someone inquire?

Contact Faith through her official channels for private discussion, press or
background context, partnership review, or future pilot consideration.
