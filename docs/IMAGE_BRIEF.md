# Image Brief

## Recommendation

Image pack needed: yes.

Big Stick should not use private screenshots as public visuals. Use original
abstract images that communicate protected command, public/private boundaries,
archive retrieval, transcript review, and redaction without showing real
records, private names, case details, IDs, addresses, or operational screens.

## Style Direction

Sharp, protective, tactical, and calm. Use a high-contrast command-board visual
language with warm paper, graphite, deep ink, restrained red, and muted gold.

Avoid militarized imagery, gavels, scales-of-justice cliches, surveillance
imagery, exposed documents, real screenshots, and anything implying public app
access.
