# Big Stick

Protected public-record and evidence command surface.

Big Stick is Faith Cheltenham's protected local command center for organizing
public-record work, private evidence review, archive retrieval, transcript
intake, AI-assisted drafts, and export readiness.

## Hero

Big Stick keeps the public story and private engine separate.

CTA: Private inquiry through Faith's official channels.

## What This Project Is

Big Stick is a protected command surface for serious documentation work. It
organizes public-record context, private review, archive retrieval, listening
and transcript intake, AI-assisted drafts, and export readiness without turning
raw private material into public content.

## Why It Matters

Some records need to be understood publicly. Some records need to stay private.
Big Stick treats that boundary as the product.

## How It Works

Public-safe context and private records move through review, redaction,
approval, and receipt checks. Approved summaries can become public-facing
material. Raw records and operational workflows remain protected.
