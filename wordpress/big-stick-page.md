# Big Stick

Protected public-record and evidence command surface.

Big Stick is Faith Cheltenham's protected local command center for organizing
public-record work, private evidence review, archive retrieval, transcript
intake, AI-assisted drafts, and export readiness.

## What This Is

Big Stick keeps public-facing material and private work in separate lanes. It
supports a public record area while preserving private evidence, transcripts,
archive material, review receipts, and operational workflows behind protected
boundaries.

## Why It Matters

Some work needs to become public. Some work must stay private. Big Stick is
built around that distinction: review before release, redaction before
publication, and receipts before claims.

## What Is Public

- A high-level project story.
- Public-safe descriptions.
- Abstract visual direction.
- Selected status language after review.

## What Remains Private

- Source code.
- Private workflows and prompts.
- Raw evidence, records, transcripts, and archive files.
- Legal/admin/medical/family details.
- Credentials, deployment details, private receipts, and operational strategy.
