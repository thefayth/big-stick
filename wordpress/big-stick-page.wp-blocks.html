Big Stick

Protected public-record and evidence command surface.

Big Stick is Faith Cheltenham's protected local command center for organizing public-record work, private evidence review, archive retrieval, transcript intake, AI-assisted drafts, and export readiness.

What This Is

Big Stick keeps public-facing material and private work in separate lanes. It supports a public record area while preserving private evidence, transcripts, archive material, review receipts, and operational workflows behind protected boundaries.

Why It Matters

Some work needs to become public. Some work must stay private. Big Stick is built around that distinction: review before release, redaction before publication, and receipts before claims.
